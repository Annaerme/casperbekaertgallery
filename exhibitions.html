Gallery in the Distillery

6–8 & 14–15 March 2026  ·  Filliers Distillery

Art met craft. Contemporary works sparked curiosity, celebrated the space and offered inspiring experiences — transforming the Filliers Distillery into a site of imagination and discovery. 1.200 visitors over two weekends.

With

Alex Brassart, Arno Camps, Jan De Lauré, Jan Vindevogel, Jeroen Frateur, Manon Soetens, Martha Onderwater, Randoald Sabbe, Stief De Smet, William Ludwig Lutgens

Gallery in the Bakery

10–12 October 2025  ·  Bakkerij Bekaert

Art and a unique space came together in an accessible, one-of-a-kind experience. The bakery's architecture — cold storage, ovens, flour-dusted walls — became the conceptual foundation. 750 visitors over three days.

With

Alex Brassart, Cas Reynders, Edo Maas, Emile Desweemer, Jean De Groote, Orlando Gruwez, Randoald Sabbe
